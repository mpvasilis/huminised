# Boost Your Team's Productivity

Most productivity tools just add more buttons. Ours cuts steps instead.

You write a task once. It routes itself, pulls in the right people, and gets out of your way. Solo founder or a team of fifty, the setup is the same: about ten minutes, and most of that is you deciding what to hand off.

We measured it. New teams hit their first finished workflow 10-20 percent faster than they did on their old stack.

So skip the demo. Start a free project and push a real task through it. If it hasn't saved you an afternoon by the end of week one, close the tab.
